Agaram Power Solution is a leading company in the renewable energy sector, specializing in the design, manufacture, and installation of solar power systems and related products. Our range of services includes Solar Power Plants, Solar Water Pumps, Solar Street Lights, Solar Water Heaters, Solar LED Lighting, and LED Name Boards.

Internship and Training Program

At Agaram Power Solution, we are committed to nurturing the next generation of solar energy professionals. Our internship and training program offer students hands-on experience and practical knowledge in the field of renewable energy. Participants will have the opportunity to work alongside experienced professionals on real-world projects, gaining insights into the latest technologies and industry practices.

Program Highlights:

Comprehensive training covering various aspects of solar energy systems Practical experience in the design, installation, and maintenance of solar power systems Exposure to a diverse range of projects, including Solar Power Plants, Street Lights, Water Pumps, and more Mentorship from industry experts to guide students throughout the program Networking opportunities with professionals and peers in the renewable energy sector Certificate of completion upon fulfilling program requirements Eligibility Criteria:

Students pursuing degrees in engineering, renewable energy, environmental science, or related fields Passionate individuals with a keen interest in sustainable energy solutions Strong communication and teamwork skills

Join us at Agaram Power Solution and embark on a rewarding journey towards a sustainable future in solar energy!
