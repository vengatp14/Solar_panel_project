About Us

Agaram Power Solution

Agaram power solution is one of the leading company, which was established from 2008 at India (Madurai - Tamilnadu). Agaram power solution is a renewable energy company created to provide green solutions at an affordable price and to meet the increasing energy demands of the society. We provide energy solutions to both domestic and industrial needs.

Focused on installing best quality rooftop and ground mounted photovoltaic projects. We have in-house capabilities across all aspects, including Design, Engineering, Procurement, Construction, Monitoring and Maintenance. We adhere worldwide practices across Products, Process, Technology and Safety. Provide installation work using the power tools and experienced personnel.

Our systems use state of the art products, which improves efficiency and performance. We started doing LED Signage Board in 2013, then 2016 onwards Agaram green energy is established only for LED signage board.

In our Agaram Power Solution we are providing products like, Solar Power Plant On grid, Off grid, Solar pump set, Solar water heaters.

Agaram Power Solution Solar street light manufacturer unit is constructed in Bangalore in 2021.We are distributing and installing throughout India. Our Manufacturing facility is capable to produce 5000 streetlight units every month.

Agaram students development program is organized to enhance the College student technical knowledge, providing Technical Workshop for the colleges, and providing employment guidance to young minds as full time and part time workers / Entrepreneurs.
